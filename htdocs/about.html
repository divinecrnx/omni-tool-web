About

This website was created as a group project assigned to two students; Zulhilmi Abdul Rasheed and Chai Yi Ting. For their Web Programming class, they were tasked with building a small-scale website that had to fulfill specific requirements.


When it came to deciding what the website would be about, Zulhilmi proposed that they use his hobby project Omni Tool to which Yi Ting agreed. And thus, after about four months of work, the website you see here today was born.
