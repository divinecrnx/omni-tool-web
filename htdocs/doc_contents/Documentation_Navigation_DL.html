Interface

Description

You are met with two options here; Youtube-DL and Music-DL.

Youtube-DL is mainly used to download videos.

Whereas...

Music-DL is similar to Youtube-DL except it automatically converts videos into music files for you (in mp3 format).

Both of them give you the same sub-options, which are:

  • Single
  • Non-Playlist Batch Download
  • Playlist Batch Download
  • Channel/User Download


Single

This is the simple, no-nonsense, copy-and-paste option for when you just want to download one single video.

Seriously, that's it. Just copy your video link and paste it into the field and you're good to go.

Batch

Now, this is where things get a bit more tedious. But, bear with me. This is worth the trouble.

Say you want to download multiple videos. But you don't want to keep pasting single links into the single option over and over again each time waiting for it to finish. That's where this option comes in.

If you navigate to the root of Omni-Tool, you'll notice a Downloads.txt file.

Simply paste your links here by separating each of them with a line break. Once you're done, fire up the script and kick back and relax while it works its magic. Once your files are downloaded, simply navigate to the downloads folder and voila! Your files are ready.

If you'd like to review the previously downloaded batch of links, there is another file called Previous Downloads.txt within the root folder that stores your previous links. Nifty, huh?

P.S: In case the script gets interrupted during the download process, simply restart the script and it will proceed to download the files which it left off.

Channel/User

For the times when you want to go crazy and download a whole youtube channel (you daring lad), this option has got you covered.

Just get your youtube channel link, paste it into the script and let er' rip! Downloading a channel has never been so easy.

WARNING: The average youtube channel may contain more than hundreds of videos. Each of them in high quality. If you're on a slow internet connection, I advise you to reconsider, as the wait time cannot be worth it.
